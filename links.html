Links

Here are some links that are useful for gardening enthusiasts.

Planting Guides

  • Gardenate tells you what to plant at what time of year depending on your climate zone.
  • Harmony Garden Centre is where Gardener Bee buys plants and seeds. They have a blog describing what to do in the garden and when for the specific climate of southern Tasmania.

YouTube Channels

  • Self Sufficient Me is a channel about gardening run by a homesteader in Queensland, Australia. Remember to give him a big green thumbs up!
  • Parkrose Permaculture has many thoughtful videos on gardening, permaculture, animal raising, and homesteading. Based out of Portland, Oregon, in the USA.
  • Epic Gardening's mission is to help people around the world learn how to grow fresh, healthy food, no matter where they live.
