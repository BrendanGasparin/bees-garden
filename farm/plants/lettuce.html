Lettuce

Lactuca sativa: Asteraceae

Sow: Container or garden.

Sow Depth: 5-8 mm.

Plant Space: 20-30 cm (8-12 inches)

Row Space: 30-50 cm (12-20 inches)

Height: 15-30 cm (6-12 inches)

Harvest: 8-12 weeks

Companion plants: Beet, Borage, Brassicas, Carrot, Chervil, Florence Fennel, Leeks, Marigold, Onion, Strawberry, Radish.

Avoid: Celery, Parsley.

Germination Temperature: 8°C and 27°C

Lettuce comes in a variety of shapes, sizes and colours. It is very easy to grow. Lettuce is mostly grown as a leaf vegetable but is sometimes used for its stems and pods.

Sow in rows and use thinnings for salad greens.

Lettuce has shallow roots and must be watered daily in hot or dry weather to prevent bitter flavour and bolting. Providing some shade to your plants can also prevent bolting.
